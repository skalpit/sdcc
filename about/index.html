About Southern District Cricket Club, Darwin

Get to know the The Crocs

Our Story

Founded in 19xx, Southern District Cricket Club, Darwin has a rich history of fostering cricketing talent and sportsmanship in Darwin. We are more than just a cricket club; we are a community dedicated to promoting the spirit of cricket, teamwork, and fair play. From our passionate senior teams to our vibrant junior programs, we strive for excellence on and off the field.

Our home ground, Marrara Cricket Ground, is a hub for cricket enthusiasts. We welcome players of all ages and skill levels.

Our Club Motto

★★Darwin's South: Play Hard, Play Fair, Together.★★

Club Mission

To provide a welcoming and competitive environment for cricketers of all ages and abilities in Darwin, fostering skill development, sportsmanship, and a lifelong love for the game, while actively contributing to our local community.

Our Facilities

Our club boasts excellent facilities to support our players and members.

  • Well-maintained turf pitches.
  • Modern training nets for practice sessions.
  • A comfortable clubhouse with amenities.
  • Ample parking and spectator areas.
  • Modern change rooms and shower facilities.
  • Equipment storage and maintenance areas.
  • Scoreboard and electronic timing systems.
