About Southern Districts Cricket Club, Darwin

Get to know the The Crocs

Our Story

Southern Districts Cricket Club Inc. has been a proud part of the Northern Territory cricket community since its founding. Based at Gerry Wood Oval in Freds Pass Reserve, the club plays in the Darwin & Districts Cricket Competition. It offers inclusive programs for juniors, seniors, and women, fostering growth across all levels.

Southern Districts Cricket Club commenced in 1981 - fielding a D grade team. In 1982 the club became an incorporated association and in 1985, a turf wicket was established to enable the club to field an A grade team. The club entered the A grade competition in 1987.

SDCC continues to promote teamwork, development, and community spirit through the game of cricket.

Club Mission

To provide a welcoming and competitive environment for cricketers of all ages and abilities in rural area, fostering skill development, sportsmanship, and a lifelong love for the game, while actively contributing to our local community.

Our Facilities

Our club boasts excellent facilities to support our players and members.

  • Well-maintained turf pitches.
  • Modern training nets for practice sessions.
  • A comfortable clubhouse with amenities.
  • Ample parking and spectator areas.
  • Modern change rooms and shower facilities.
  • Equipment storage and maintenance areas.
  • Scoreboard and electronic timing systems.
